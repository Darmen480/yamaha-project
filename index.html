ABOUT

We build the best 2-wheeled machines that will drive you more crazy! Yamaha was first founded by Torakusu Yamaha in Japan, year 1887. It was first established as a piano and reed organ manufacturer by Torakusu Yamaha in Hamamatsu, Japan and was incorporated on October 12, 1897. Our company is also involved in the import and sales of various types of products, development of tourist businesses and management of leisure, recreational facilities and related services. Yamaha’s motorcycle sales are the second largest in the world and Yamaha is the world leader in water vehicle sales.
